WELCOME TO CrossTalk 2025

The 2025 International Conference on Linguistics, Translation & Culture (CTLTC 2025)

Welcome to the official website of the 2025 International Conference on Linguistics, Translation & Culture (CTLTC 2025), which will be held in Sapporo, Japan, from January 10-12, 2025, at the prestigious Hokkaido University, located at Kita 8, Nishi 5, Kita-ku, Sapporo, Hokkaido, 060-0808 Japan (+81-11-716-2111).

The theme of CTLTC 2025, "Fostering Connections: Bridging Linguistics, Translation, and Culture", highlights the critical role of language as a bridge across diverse cultures and perspectives. In today's rapidly evolving global landscape, linguistics and translation play a pivotal role in shaping meaningful communication and cultural exchange. This conference aims to explore the synergies among these disciplines, uncovering innovative approaches and fostering dialogue on how they contribute to a more interconnected and inclusive world.

Join us in Sapporo, a city celebrated for its enchanting winter scenery, vibrant culture, and warm hospitality. As the largest city on Japan's northernmost island of Hokkaido, Sapporo offers a unique blend of traditional Japanese charm and modern sophistication, making it an inspiring backdrop for intellectual discourse and professional networking.

Whether you are a researcher, academic, or practitioner, CTLTC 2025 provides an excellent opportunity to engage in thought-provoking discussions, build connections with global peers, and immerse yourself in the unique cultural and natural wonders of Sapporo.

We look forward to welcoming you to Sapporo for an enriching and unforgettable experience at CTLTC 2025!

Submission

If you wish to attend our conference, please send the title and abstract of your article to CTLTC@academic.net, and our staff will send you an invitation letter after approval.

If you submit the full text, your paper will go through two rounds of review, including initial review and peer review. Usually, the entire review process takes 3-4 weeks (or longer).

If your full text is accepted, we will send a formal acceptance notice to all authors, along with an article review form. Please pay attention to the conference secretary's e-mail.

After you receive the invitation letter, our team will send you the detailed conference schedule and relevant information. We appreciate your patience and look forward to your participation

Submitted articles will be reviewed by the conference committee, and approved articles will be registered for publication in one of the following journals:

  • International Journal of Languages, Literature and Linguistics (IJLLL)
    Index: Google Scholar, Crossref, CNKI.
    ISSN: 2382 - 6282
    Frequency: Bimonthly
    DOI: 10.18178/IJLLL
  • International Journal of Social Sciences and Humanities (IJSSH)
    Index: Google Scholar, Index Copernicus, Crossref, Electronic Journals Library.
    ISSN: 2010-3646
    Frequency: Bimonthly
    DOI: 10.18178/IJSSH

Conference Fee: The conference registration fee is 20,000 JPY, which covers access to all sessions, materials, and refreshments during the conference.

Please note that accommodation will not be provided by the conference organizers. Participants are kindly requested to make their own arrangements for lodging. We appreciate your understanding and cooperation in this matter.
